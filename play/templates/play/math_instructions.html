Welcome to the challenge!

Lets see how many points you can get with in 60 seconds. This is how the game works:

  • Press "Enter" in order to start the game, it will start the clock and you can start answering.
  • You will have a math multiplication question on the top.
  • For every answer you enter that is correct, you will gain a point. Every wrong answer you will lose a point
  • To enter your answer type in the text field your answer (number) and press "Enter".
  • Notice: once you start the clock you have 60 seconds.
